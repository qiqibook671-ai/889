Independent Contractor Agreement

This Independent Contractor Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of , by and between Ideadocks LLC Toronto, a Canadian limited liability company (“Company”), located at 236 W 30th St RM 1401, New York, NY, USA, with a branch office in Toronto, Canada, and (“Contractor”), collectively referred to as the “Parties.”

1. Engagement and Services

The Company hereby engages the Contractor as an independent contractor to perform the services described below (the "Services"):

  • Uploading and optimizing product data and detail pages from various brand partners (such as Estée Lauder, Audemars Piguet, Armani, Ferragamo) onto e-commerce platforms including Amazon, eBay, Walmart.com, Shopify, and Etsy.
  • Ensuring all product information is accurate, complete, and error-free.
  • Completing assigned product data upload tasks daily between 10:00 AM and 11:00 PM Eastern Time (Toronto).
  • Helping smaller brands improve their product visibility and sales by boosting their presence on the platform.

2. Term

This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue until terminated by either party with notice.

3. Payment and Compensation

The Contractor shall receive a fixed salary paid by the Company in Canadian Dollars (CAD).

In addition, the Contractor is guaranteed a minimum daily payment of CAD $60 from Brand Partners upon completing two task sets each day.

Additional bonuses may be awarded for completing special bonus orders, holiday bonuses, and platform anniversary bonuses.

Payments will be made on the 5th, 15th, and last day of each month via PayPal, Interac e-Transfer, or other mutually agreed methods (Toronto, EST/EDT).

Tax Withholding: The commissions you earn will be reported on a T4A slip for Canadian tax purposes. The Contractor is responsible for remitting any applicable taxes to the Canada Revenue Agency.

3.1 Deposit and Combined Tasks

The Contractor shall provide a refundable deposit of CAD $135 as a performance guarantee to accept Brand Partner tasks. This deposit can be withdrawn freely only after the Contractor has completed 7 consecutive working days.

Certain tasks may be Combined Tasks, consisting of 1 to 3 orders. Completion of Combined Tasks may result in the Contractor's account balance becoming negative and exceeding the initial deposit.

In such cases, the Contractor is responsible for clearing any negative balances to maintain a positive account status.

Upon successfully clearing any negative balance related to a Combined Task, the Contractor shall be entitled to a profit bonus equal to 20 times the value of the common task within the Combined Task.

3.2 Milestone Payments

  • 5 days: CAD $1,200
  • 15 days: CAD $2,000
  • 30 days: CAD $5,000

4. Independent Contractor Status

The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, or representative of the Company.

The Contractor shall have no authority to bind the Company in any manner.

The Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits.

5. Confidentiality

The Contractor agrees to keep confidential all proprietary information, client data, business practices, and other non-public information obtained during the term of this Agreement.

This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

6. Work Schedule and Performance

The Contractor shall log into the official platform daily between 10:00 AM and 11:00 PM Eastern Time (Toronto) to complete assigned tasks.

Work hours are flexible as long as tasks are completed correctly and on schedule.

The Contractor agrees to perform the Services with the highest professional standards.

Failure to complete assigned tasks on time or leaving orders unfinished will negatively affect the Contractor’s reputation score in the platform system, potentially impacting their ability to receive further work.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with notice.

Upon termination, the Contractor shall return all Company property and confidential information.

8. Miscellaneous

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada.

If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
